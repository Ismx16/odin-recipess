Chorizo Sloppy Joes

These Chorizo Sloppy Joes are a flavorful twist on the classic sloppy joe sandwich. Made with spicy chorizo sausage, tangy tomato sauce, and a blend of seasonings, they are sure to be a hit at your next meal.

  • Ingredients
  • 1 1/4 pounds bulk fresh Mexican chorizo
  • 1 1/4 pounds bulk fresh Mexican chorizo
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 sesame seed hamburger buns, split and toasted
  • 1 large avocado, thinly sliced
  • 3/4 cup corn chips (such as Fritos), roughly crushed if neededd
