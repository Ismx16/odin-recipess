Creamy Cavatappi

This creamy cavatappi recipe is a delicious and comforting pasta dish that's perfect for any night of the week. The combination of creamy sauce, tender pasta, and savory seasonings makes it a family favorite.

  • Ingredients:
  • 8 ounces cavatappi pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnishh (optional)
